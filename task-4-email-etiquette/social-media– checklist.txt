 Responsible Social Media Use – Checklist


 Do’s
Think before you post — ensure content is respectful and appropriate
Keep your privacy settings updated and secure
Verify information before sharing to prevent misinformation
Use clear and professional language when needed
Be respectful and responsible in all online interactions



 Don’ts
Don’t post content that can harm your reputation
Don’t share sensitive personal information (address, phone, passwords)
Don’t engage in cyberbullying, hate, or trolling
Don’t accept unknown requests without verification
Don’t overshare your live location or daily routine
