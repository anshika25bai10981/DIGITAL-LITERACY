## Overview

This repository contains two formal emails demonstrating proper email etiquette, including clear subject lines, professional tone, and structured writing.

## Emails Included

1. Assignment Extension Request

Email to a professor requesting a deadline extension due to hackathon participation.

2. Summer Internship Application
   
Email to an internship coordinator expressing interest in a summer internship.

## Learning Outcomes

--Professional email writing

--Clear communication

--Proper structure and tone
