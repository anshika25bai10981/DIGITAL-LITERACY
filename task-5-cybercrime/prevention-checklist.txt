## Stay Safe Online – Student Cyber Safety Checklist


1. Smart Safety Habits
Turn on 2-step verification for extra account protection
Use different passwords for email, social media, and banking
Pause before you click — suspicious links = biggest threat
Install apps only from official stores (Play Store/App Store)
Keep your phone and laptop regularly updated
Avoid logging into personal accounts on shared/public systems


2. UPI & Financial Safety 
Double-check UPI IDs and QR codes before sending money
Never approve a “collect request” unless you initiated the payment
Remember: Receiving money does NOT require entering a PIN


3. Extra Protection Tips
Avoid free/public Wi-Fi for payments or logins
Limit what you share — your data is valuable
Backup important files to cloud or external storage


4. Report Immediately

 --Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
 --Helpline: 1930


Stay alert. Stay informed. One careless click can cost more than you think.
