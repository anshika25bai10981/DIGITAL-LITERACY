# Project Overview

This project was completed as part of the Digital Literacy (CSE0001) course. It is designed to build and strengthen essential digital skills required in today’s academic and professional environment. The project includes activities such as designing presentations, creating a professional digital portfolio, exploring coding and collaboration platforms, understanding proper email and social media etiquette, and learning about cyber safety. Through these modules, I developed hands-on experience with various digital tools and understood the importance of maintaining a responsible and professional online identity.

# Module Summary

## Module 1: Presentation Skills
In this module, I worked on creating a digital literacy infographic using tools like Canva or Prezi. The focus was on presenting information in a clear, engaging, and visually appealing manner. It helped me understand design principles and effective communication techniques.

## Module 2: Digital Portfolio Development
This module involved building and updating professional profiles on platforms such as GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle. It highlighted the importance of creating a strong online presence and showcasing skills and achievements in a professional way.

## Module 3: Programming & Collaboration
Here, I practiced coding on platforms like HackerRank and CodeChef by solving beginner-level problems. Additionally, I created a Google Form quiz to spread awareness about digital literacy.
Quiz Link: https://forms.gle/JwCchMnr2nc7J9Lk7

## Module 4: Email & Social Media Etiquette
This module focused on writing professional emails, including requests for assignments and internship inquiries. I also learned the correct structure, tone, and etiquette for emails. Along with this, I prepared a checklist of appropriate and inappropriate practices for social media usage.

## Module 5: Cybercrime Awareness & Financial Safety
